Drosophila and Endogenous Viral Elements (EVEs)

🧬

What Are Viral Fossils?

Endogenous Viral Elements (EVEs) are ancient viral DNA sequences that have become permanently integrated into the genomes of living organisms. They're like fossils written in our genetic code.

8%

8% of Human DNA

About 8% of the human genome consists of viral remnants from infections our ancestors survived millions of years ago.

🪰

Fruit Flies as Detectives

Drosophila melanogaster shares 60% of its genes with humans, making it the perfect model to study how EVEs evolve and affect health.

🛡️

Ancient Immunity

Some EVEs have been repurposed to help fight new viruses, acting like "wanted posters" that recognize and block related pathogens.

Advanced Level: Antiviral Immunity Challenge

Use EVE type and state to predict protection against invading viruses. Functional EVEs provide sequence-specific immunity; matching type matters (retrovirus EVEs protect against retroviruses). Degraded EVEs offer no protection; silent EVEs can be reactivated; EVE evolution mirrors host evolution, but function can be lost or gained.
