🔴 RED DECODER CARDS
🔴 HOW TO PLAY - RED DECODER
1️⃣ Cut out the red decoder film or use red cellophane.
2️⃣ Slide the red decoder over the HUNT card. Red dots disappear! Only the dark teal EVEs remain visible.
3️⃣ Mark your finds, then flip to the ANSWER card to check the colours and types.
💡 Science: Red filter blocks red light, making red dummy dots invisible. Dark teal EVEs become obvious!
