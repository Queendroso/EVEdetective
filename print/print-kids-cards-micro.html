🔴 RED DECODER CARDS
🔴 HOW TO PLAY - RED DECODER VERSION
1️⃣ Cut out the red decoder film (included separately) or use red cellophane.
2️⃣ Slide the red decoder over the HUNT card. Red dots disappear! Only the real EVEs remain visible.
3️⃣ Mark your finds, then flip to the ANSWER card to see the true colours and shapes.

💡 Science behind it: Red filter blocks red light, so red dummy dots vanish. Cyan/teal EVEs become dark and obvious!
