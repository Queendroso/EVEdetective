🧬 How to Play

1️⃣ Give each player or team the 5 species cards.

2️⃣ Compare the coloured barcode bands on each card. Same colour at the same position = shared EVE.

3️⃣ Place the two most similar cards (most shared bands) in Pair A.

4️⃣ Place the next two most similar cards in Pair B.

5️⃣ Place the odd one out (least shared bands) in Outgroup.

6️⃣ Use the answer key on the tree mat to check your answers!
